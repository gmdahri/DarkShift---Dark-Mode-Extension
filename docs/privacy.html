Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 9, 2024

TL;DR - We Don't Collect Data

DarkShift does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever. All settings are stored locally on your device. Your privacy is 100% protected.

Overview

DarkShift ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome extension.

Data Collection

DarkShift does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever.

All settings, preferences, and configurations are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API and never leave your computer.

Information Storage

What We Store Locally:

  • Dark mode on/off state for each website
  • Custom brightness, contrast, warmth, and grayscale settings
  • Saved presets and themes
  • Whitelist and blacklist of domains
  • User interface preferences

Where It's Stored:

All data is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage using Chrome's built-in storage API. This data:

  • Remains on your device
  • Is never transmitted to external servers
  • Is not accessible to us or any third parties
  • Can be deleted by removing the extension

Permissions Explanation

DarkShift requires the following permissions to function:

1. "Access your data on all websites"

Why: To inject dark mode CSS filters and styling onto web pages you visit.

What we do: Apply visual transformations (dark mode) to websites.

What we DON'T do: Read, collect, or transmit any content from websites.

2. "Storage"

Why: To save your preferences and settings locally on your device.

What we do: Store your dark mode preferences for each website.

What we DON'T do: Send this data anywhere or share it with anyone.

3. "Active Tab"

Why: To know which website you're currently viewing to apply the correct settings.

What we do: Check the URL to load the appropriate dark mode settings.

What we DON'T do: Track your browsing history or send tab information anywhere.

Third-Party Services

DarkShift does NOT use:

  • ❌ Analytics services (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • ❌ Advertising networks
  • ❌ Social media tracking pixels
  • ❌ External APIs or servers
  • ❌ Error reporting services that transmit data
  • ❌ Any form of telemetry or usage tracking

Children's Privacy

DarkShift does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we don't collect data at all, the extension is safe for all ages.

Open Source

DarkShift is open source. You can review the complete source code to verify our privacy practices:

GitHub Repository: https://github.com/gmdahri/DarkShift---Dark-Mode-Extension

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Data Security

Since we don't collect or transmit any data, there is no data to secure beyond what is already protected by your browser's local storage mechanisms.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • View your settings in the extension popup
  • Export your presets and configurations
  • Delete all data by uninstalling the extension
  • Clear settings by resetting to defaults

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or DarkShift's privacy practices:

  • GitHub Issues: Report an issue
  • Repository: View source code

Compliance

DarkShift complies with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

Since we don't collect data, there is no data to process, share, or delete.

Summary

In short: DarkShift respects your privacy completely. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Everything stays on your device. We can't see what websites you visit, what you browse, or how you use the extension.

Your privacy is paramount. We built DarkShift to enhance your browsing experience, not to track you.
